Growing Together: Weekly Check-In

A simple 15-minute ritual to stay connected, celebrate progress, and keep your relationship growing.

🌟
Step one

Appreciation

Share one thing you appreciated about your partner this week. Be specific. It does not need to be big.

"Something I appreciated about you this week was..."
🌡️
Step two

Temperature Check

On a scale of 1 to 10, how connected did you feel this week? Share your number and a brief reason why.

"This week I felt about a ___ because..."
💚
Step three

One Request

Is there one small thing that would help you feel more connected next week? Keep it simple and actionable.

"Something that would help me feel more connected is..."

Monthly reflection

  • 📖 What did we handle well this month?
  • 🔄 What would we do differently?
  • 📈 How have we grown since we started?
  • 🎯 What do we want to focus on next month?

Journalling prompts

  • ✏️ How did the relationship feel this week?
  • ✅ What went well between us?
  • 💭 What was hard, and why?
  • 🌱 What do I want more of?

Make it a habit

Pick a regular time and put it in the calendar. Treat it like any other important appointment. Even 15 minutes makes a difference.

Remember

The strongest relationships are not the ones without problems. They are the ones where both partners keep choosing to show up, learn, and grow together.
